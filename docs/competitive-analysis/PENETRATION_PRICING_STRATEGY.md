## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Competitor Price Matching

**Risk:** TaxDome/Karbon drop prices to match Accute

**Mitigation:**
- Accute still has AI Psychology Profiling (unique, not replicable quickly)
- Multi-agent orchestration (11 agents vs 0-2 for competitors)
- 95/100 AI score vs 38-46/100 for competitors
- Even at same price, Accute is technically superior

### Risk 2: Cash Flow Pressure

**Risk:** Low margins + high growth = cash burn

**Mitigation:**
- Incentivize annual/3-year billing (upfront cash)
- 22% of customers on annual = 12 months cash upfront
- Monitor burn rate monthly, adjust pricing if needed
- Exit strategy: Raise prices in Year 2 if margins drop below 12%

### Risk 3: Cost Overruns

**Risk:** Actual costs exceed $7/$18/$30 projections

**Mitigation:**
- LLM cost controls: usage limits on free/low tiers
- Prompt optimization to reduce token usage
- Reserved AWS instances (30% savings)
- Monitor cost per user weekly
- Adjust pricing if costs spike 20%+

### Risk 4: Low Conversion Rate

**Risk:** Trials don't convert to paid customers

**Mitigation:**
- 14-day free trial (no credit card required)
- Onboarding automation (Day 1 tasks, nudges)
- Target 30% trial-to-paid conversion
- If conversion <20%, add more onboarding support

### Risk 5: High Churn

**Risk:** Customers cancel after 1-3 months

**Mitigation:**
- Psychology profiling creates switching costs (data lock-in)
- AI agents become embedded in daily workflows
- Target <5% monthly churn (industry standard: 3-7%)
- Proactive customer success for at-risk accounts
