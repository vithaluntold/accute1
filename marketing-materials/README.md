## Common Questions & Answers

### "Can Accute handle both use cases?"
**Yes!** The platform is the same. The positioning and messaging differ based on buyer needs and competitive landscape.

### "Should we pursue both markets simultaneously?"
**Recommended approach:**
- **Phase 1 (Months 1-6):** Focus on Professional Practices (clearer pain, faster sales cycle)
- **Phase 2 (Months 7-12):** Expand to Pan-Industry (leverage case studies from Phase 1)

### "What if a prospect spans both categories?"
Example: Law firm that also wants to automate HR processes
- **Lead with:** Professional Practices positioning (their primary identity)
- **Expand to:** Pan-Industry capabilities for HR automation
- **Use:** Both sets of materials as appropriate
