## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is there a contract?**  
A: Annual billing requires a 12-month commitment. Monthly billing is month-to-month with 30-day notice to cancel.

**Q: What happens if we exceed 100 GB storage (Professional tier)?**  
A: You'll receive a notification at 80% and 95% usage. You can purchase additional storage blocks or upgrade to Enterprise (unlimited).

**Q: Can we mix Professional and Enterprise licenses?**  
A: Yes. For example, partners/managers on Enterprise (priority support) and staff on Professional.

**Q: How does user counting work?**  
A: Any person who logs in = 1 user. Client portal users are unlimited and free.

**Q: Can we start small and scale up?**  
A: Absolutely. Start with one team (5-10 users), expand to department, then firm-wide. Add users anytime.

**Q: What's included in onboarding?**  
A: Professional tier: online training, documentation, email support. Enterprise tier: dedicated onboarding specialist, custom training sessions, account setup.

**Q: Can we cancel mid-year?**  
A: Annual contracts are non-refundable, but you can choose not to renew. Monthly billing can cancel with 30-day notice.

**Q: Do you offer non-profit or academic discounts?**  
A: Yes. Contact sales for 25% non-profit/academic discount eligibility.

**Q: What's your data retention policy?**  
A: Active subscription: indefinite. After cancellation: 90-day grace period, then data deleted. Enterprise+: custom retention available.
